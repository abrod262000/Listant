What is Listant?

Posted by James Palasz on March 29, 2016

Listant is a peer-to-peer marketplace for freelance work. This means that Listant provides the connections in your community for those who are looking to outsource work, with those who are looking to work...and vice versa!

From mowing your neighbor’s lawn to getting your garage cleaned out, the opportunities are endless.

When you post a job, members of your community looking for work in that field are instantly alerted through notifications. They can also view the Listant marketplace. Community members will apply to your job and offer you a price based on your desired amount. You then have the power to choose the applicant that best suits your job description.

On the other end, Listant enables workers to be their own boss. When you want to work, you can be notified by a specific type of job. If you don’t know where to start, you can find job ideas in the Listant marketplace. You can apply to these jobs and offer your desired wage. Once you are accepted, you are ready to work. Once you are done, you are paid.

Listant provides all of the resources that you will need to complete this process: the platform to make the connection, a messenger for communication, and a payment processor that allows you to make & accept payments.

Listant’s unique platform allows you to make your life easier than ever before. Start freeing up your schedule and padding your wallet with Listant TODAY!
