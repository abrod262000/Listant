Improve Your Community

Posted by James Palasz on April 4, 2016

We are always looking for ways to improve and give back to the communities we live in. Doing daily things on a small scale can go a long way when it comes to showing the world how great your community is! Here are some ideas to get you started:

  1. Help a Neighbor: Everyone needs a helping hand sometimes, and sometimes the smallest gestures are the most meaningful. When Spring comes around, help your neighbor with some yard clean up. When fall arrives, you can assist with raking leaves.

  2. Visit Your Local Farmer’s Market: Visiting your local farmers not only boost your local economy, but also benefits you directly! Shopping at the farmers market allows you to enjoy nutrient-rich foods and have a better understanding of where your food is coming from!

  3. Volunteer: From joining a clean up group to serving meals at a shelter, the opportunities to volunteer your time and make a difference are endless!

  4. Welcome New Neighbors: Moving into a new home in a new town is not an easy transition, so help your neighbors feel a little more welcome with some food, a helping hand, or just a simple “Welcome to the neighborhood!”

  5. Clean Up Litter: You don’t have to join a group or an initiative to clean up your community, just take the extra step and clean up some garbage during your daily commute!

  6. Keep Your House In Shape: Whether it is leaving the porch light on at night or pulling the weeds on your curb, a couple minutes of exterior home improvement not only makes your home look better, but also the neighborhood as a whole!

  7. Share Positive Community News: There is so much happening in your community every day that it is difficult to keep track! Stay up to date with local newspapers and other news outlets and share events and stories that show how great your community is!

These are just a few ways to start improving your community. Taking a few minutes out of your day for even the smallest gestures can make a big impact!
