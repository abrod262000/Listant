How To Get Hired

Posted by Dillon Temple on April 26, 2016

We want you to be able to get hired every single time you apply! However, being hired for a Listant job can be competitive at times. Here are some tips that will put you a notch above the rest of the field!

  1. Have a profile picture : Let the person who posted the job know who you are! Having a picture adds to your credibility and gives the connection a more personal feel.
  2. Have an awesome profile bio : The outsourcer is going to review several applicants...what is going to make you stand out? Put some time into crafting a unique bio that will highlight your skills and your personality!
  3. Be an exceptional worker : Just like any other job: show up on time and do a complete job. This will help you receive great ratings and even better tips!
  4. Offer a realistic price : It’s no secret that outsourcers will try to pay the least amount possible to get their job done. However, having a great Listant rating can allow you to raise your wages to match your proven work ethic. Take advantage of that!
  5. Make a great application message : Whether it is proving that you are valid, or even just a simple “Can’t wait to get to work!” reach out to the outsourcer in some way that will make you a more desirable hire than the competition!
  6. Know your filters : Don’t spend your time looking through jobs that don’t interest you! Use the filters on the top right corner of your screen to show you the jobs that you want!
  7. Read Listant publications : We are always generating insightful content that will help you improve your Listant experience, so stay up to date with our publications for the latest promotions, news, updates, and more!

Remember, when you can’t find a job that fits in your schedule, don’t stress for an instant, Just Listant!
